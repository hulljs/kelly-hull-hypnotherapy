Common Questions

What You're Wondering About Hypnotherapy

Will I lose control during hypnosis?
No, this is the most common misconception about hypnotherapy. You are always aware of your surroundings, always in control, and always able to stop the session at any time. Hypnosis is a state of focused relaxation, not unconsciousness.
Can anyone be hypnotized?
Almost everyone can experience hypnosis to some degree. All that is required is a willingness to relax and follow gentle guidance. People who are highly imaginative often respond especially well.
How many sessions will I need?
It depends on the issue and the person. Many clients experience significant change in 1–3 sessions. More complex or long-standing issues may benefit from 4–6 sessions. Kelly will give you an honest assessment after your free consultation.
Does online hypnotherapy really work?
Yes, research and client experience consistently show that online hypnotherapy is as effective as in-person sessions. Many clients find it easier to relax in their own home environment.
Is hypnotherapy a substitute for therapy or medication?
Hypnotherapy is a powerful complementary tool, but it does not replace medical or psychiatric care. Kelly works collaboratively alongside other practitioners and will always recommend professional support when appropriate.
What states do you serve online?
Kelly currently serves online clients in most U.S. states where hypnotherapy does not require additional licensure. Please contact her if you have a question about your specific state. She is happy to verify before booking.
